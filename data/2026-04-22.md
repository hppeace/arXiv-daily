### [1] [Match-Any-Events: Zero-Shot Motion-Robust Feature Matching Across Wide Baselines for Event Cameras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18744)
*Ruijun Zhang, Hang Su, Kostas Daniilidis, Ziyun Wang*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了首个实现跨数据集宽基线零样本匹配的事件相机匹配模型，通过运动鲁棒的注意力骨干网络和大规模事件运动合成框架，在无需目标域微调的情况下显著提升了事件特征匹配性能。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 事件相机在瞬时运动估计中展现出潜力，但任意视角间的宽基线对应关系计算仍面临重大挑战，因为事件外观随运动变化显著，且基于学习的方法受限于可扩展性和有限的宽基线监督数据。

**Method:** 该方法引入了一个运动鲁棒且计算高效的注意力骨干网络，从事件流中学习多时间尺度特征，并辅以稀疏感知的事件令牌选择机制；同时开发了一个鲁棒的事件运动合成框架，用于生成具有增强视角、模态和运动的大规模事件匹配数据集。

**Result:** 在多个基准测试上的广泛实验表明，该框架相比先前最佳的事件特征匹配方法实现了37.7%的性能提升，且单个训练模型能够在未见数据集上以零样本方式部署而无需目标域微调或适应。

**Conclusion:** 该研究首次实现了事件相机的跨数据集宽基线零样本匹配，通过创新的网络架构和合成数据生成方法解决了事件匹配中的监督数据稀缺和泛化能力不足问题，为事件相机的实际应用提供了重要技术基础。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Event cameras have recently shown promising capabilities in instantaneous motion estimation due to their robustness to low light and fast motions. However, computing wide-baseline correspondence between two arbitrary views remains a significant challenge, since event appearance changes substantially with motion, and learning-based approaches are constrained by both scalability and limited wide-baseline supervision. We therefore introduce the first event matching model that achieves cross-dataset wide-baseline correspondence in a zero-shot manner: a single model trained once is deployed on unseen datasets without any target-domain fine-tuning or adaptation. To enable this capability, we introduce a motion-robust and computationally efficient attention backbone that learns multi-timescale features from event streams, augmented with sparsity-aware event token selection, making large-scale training on diverse wide-baseline supervision computationally feasible. To provide the supervision needed for wide-baseline generalization, we develop a robust event motion synthesis framework to generate large-scale event-matching datasets with augmented viewpoints, modalities, and motions. Extensive experiments across multiple benchmarks show that our framework achieves a 37.7% improvement over the previous best event feature matching methods. Code and data are available at: https://github.com/spikelab-jhu/Match-Any-Events.

### [2] [SpikeMLLM: Spike-based Multimodal Large Language Models via Modality-Specific Temporal Scales and Temporal Compression](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18610)
*Han Xu, Zhiyong Qin, Di Shang, Jiahong Zhang, Xuerui Qiu, Bo Lei, Tiejun Huang, Bo Xu, Guoqi Li*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了SpikeMLLM，首个基于脉冲神经网络的MLLM框架，通过模态特定时间尺度和时间压缩LIF机制，在保持性能的同时大幅降低计算开销，并展示了算法-硬件协同设计在高效多模态智能中的潜力。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 多模态大语言模型在推理过程中产生巨大的计算开销和能耗，限制了其在资源受限环境中的部署；而脉冲神经网络虽然具有事件驱动的计算优势，但扩展到MLLM面临两个关键挑战：异构模态使得统一脉冲编码不足，高分辨率图像输入放大了时间步展开的开销。

**Method:** 提出了SpikeMLLM框架，在脉冲表示空间中统一现有ANN量化方法，并引入了基于模态演化差异的模态特定时间尺度，以及时间压缩LIF机制，将时间步从T=L-1压缩到T=log2(L)-1，有效处理多模态输入的计算效率问题。

**Result:** 在四个代表性MLLM和多样化多模态基准测试中，SpikeMLLM在激进的时间步压缩下保持接近无损的性能，在InternVL2-8B和Qwen2VL-72B上相对于FP16基线的平均差距仅为0.72%和1.19%；专用RTL加速器在部署导向的协同设计设置下，相比FP16 GPU基线实现了9.06倍吞吐量和25.8倍能效提升。

**Conclusion:** SpikeMLLM证明了将脉冲神经网络扩展到多模态大语言模型的可行性，通过算法-硬件协同设计实现了显著的能效提升，为资源受限环境中的高效多模态智能部署提供了有前景的解决方案，展示了脉冲计算在多模态AI中的实际应用价值。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) have achieved remarkable progress but incur substantial computational overhead and energy consumption during inference, limiting deployment in resource-constrained environments. Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs), with their sparse event-driven computation, offer inherent energy efficiency advantages on neuromorphic hardware, yet extending them to MLLMs faces two key challenges: heterogeneous modalities make uniform spike encoding insufficient, and high-resolution image inputs amplify timestep unfolding overhead. We propose SpikeMLLM, the first spike-based framework for MLLMs, which unifies existing ANN quantization methods in the spiking representation space and incorporates Modality-Specific Temporal Scales (MSTS) guided by Modality Evolution Discrepancy (MED) and Temporally Compressed LIF (TC-LIF) for timestep compression from T=L-1 to T=log2(L)-1. Experiments on four representative MLLMs across diverse multimodal benchmarks show that SpikeMLLM maintains near-lossless performance under aggressive timestep compression (Tv/Tt=3/4), with average gaps of only 0.72% and 1.19% relative to the FP16 baseline on InternVL2-8B and Qwen2VL-72B. We further develop a dedicated RTL accelerator tailored to the spike-driven datapath, observing 9.06x higher throughput and 25.8x better power efficiency relative to an FP16 GPU baseline under a deployment-oriented co-design setting, suggesting the promise of algorithm-hardware co-design for efficient multimodal intelligence.


### [3] [Neuromorphic Continual Learning for Sequential Deployment of Nuclear Plant Monitoring Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.18611)
*Samrendra Roy, Sajedul Talukder, Syed Bahauddin Alam*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了首个基于脉冲神经网络的核工业控制系统异常检测系统，采用持续学习方法解决灾难性遗忘问题，通过脉冲编码异步传感器融合实现高能效监控，在核安全数据集上达到0.979的平均F1分数和接近零的平均遗忘率。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 核工业控制系统需要跨多个子系统进行连续、高能效的异常监测，但传统神经网络在顺序学习新子系统时会出现灾难性遗忘问题，这在安全关键应用中是不可接受的，因此需要开发能够同时解决遗忘和能效挑战的持续学习系统。

**Method:** 该方法采用基于脉冲神经网络的异常检测系统，引入脉冲编码异步传感器融合技术，将异构传感器流转换为稀疏脉冲序列，实现92.7%的输入稀疏度；评估了五种持续学习策略，包括顺序微调、弹性权重巩固、突触智能、经验回放以及混合EWC+Replay方法，在HAI 21.03核安全数据集上对三个顺序部署的子系统进行测试。

**Result:** 混合EWC+Replay方法在三个顺序部署子系统上达到平均F1分数0.979，平均遗忘率接近零（单种子AF=0.000，三种子平均AF=0.035±0.039），相比等效人工神经网络减少12.6倍操作次数和估计2.5倍能耗，系统检测所有攻击的平均延迟为0.6秒。

**Conclusion:** 研究表明神经形态计算为下一代核设施提供了可行的持续监控路径，通过脉冲神经网络和持续学习策略的结合，实现了高能效、低遗忘的异常检测系统，为安全关键工业控制系统的自适应监控开辟了新方向。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Anomaly detection in nuclear industrial control systems (ICS) requires continuous, energy-efficient monitoring across multiple subsystems that are often deployed at different stages of plant commissioning. When a conventional neural network is sequentially trained to monitor new subsystems, it catastrophically forgets previously learned anomaly patterns, a safety-critical failure mode. We present the first spiking neural network (SNN)-based anomaly detection system with continual learning for nuclear ICS, addressing both challenges simultaneously. Our approach introduces spike-encoded asynchronous sensor fusion, a delta-based encoding that converts heterogeneous sensor streams into sparse spike trains at rates dictated by each sensor's natural dynamics, achieving 92.7% input sparsity. We evaluate five continual learning strategies, including sequential fine-tuning, Elastic Weight Consolidation (EWC), Synaptic Intelligence (SI), experience replay, and a hybrid EWC+Replay approach, on the HAI 21.03 nuclear ICS security dataset across three sequentially deployed subsystems (boiler, turbine, water treatment). The hybrid EWC+Replay method achieves an average F1 score of 0.979 with near-zero average forgetting (AF = 0.000 single seed; 0.035 +/- 0.039 across three seeds), while requiring 12.6x fewer operations (an estimated 2.5x in energy based on published hardware specifications) than an equivalent artificial neural network. The system detects all tested attacks with a mean latency of 0.6 seconds. These results demonstrate that neuromorphic computing offers a viable path toward always-on, energy-efficient, and adaptable safety monitoring for next-generation nuclear facilities.
