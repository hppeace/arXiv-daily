[1] Fluently Lying: Adversarial Robustness Can Be Substrate-Dependent

Daye Kang, Hyeongboo Baek

🧩 TL;DR

该研究首次报告了对抗性攻击中检测数量保持而精度崩溃的"质量腐化"现象，揭示了现有防御工具依赖于检测数量与精度同步下降的假设存在缺陷，并证明对抗性失效模式具有底物依赖性。

📘 Detailed Summary

Motivation: 现有监控和防御目标检测器对抗攻击的工具假设检测精度下降时检测数量会同步减少，但这种耦合关系仅是假设而未经测量验证，研究旨在探索这一假设的有效性并识别可能的反例。

Method: 研究采用EMS-YOLO这一脉冲神经网络目标检测器作为主要研究对象，在标准PGD攻击下进行测试，同时评估了四种不同SNN架构在两种威胁模型（l-∞和l-2范数）下的表现，并测试了五种标准防御组件对质量腐化现象的检测和缓解能力。

Result: 实验发现EMS-YOLO在标准PGD攻击下出现质量腐化现象：检测数量保持70%以上而mAP从0.528崩溃至0.042；该现象仅在四种SNN检测器中的EMS-YOLO上出现，且所有五种标准防御组件均无法检测或缓解此现象，表明防御生态系统可能依赖于针对单一底物校准的共享假设。

Conclusion: 研究首次提供了对抗性失效模式具有底物依赖性的证据，揭示了现有防御工具基于检测数量与精度同步下降假设的局限性，质量腐化现象表明需要开发更全面的防御策略来应对不同类型的对抗性攻击失效模式。

📄 Abstract

The primary tools used to monitor and defend object detectors under adversarial attack assume that when accuracy degrades, detection count drops in tandem. This coupling was assumed, not measured. We report a counterexample observed on a single model: under standard PGD, EMS-YOLO, a spiking neural network (SNN) object detector, retains more than 70% of its detections while mAP collapses from 0.528 to 0.042. We term this count-preserving accuracy collapse Quality Corruption (QC), to distinguish it from the suppression that dominates untargeted evaluation. Across four SNN architectures and two threat models (l-infinity and l-2), QC appears only in one of the four detectors tested (EMS-YOLO). On this model, all five standard defense components fail to detect or mitigate QC, suggesting the defense ecosystem may rely on a shared assumption calibrated on a single substrate. These results provide, to our knowledge, the first evidence that adversarial failure modes can be substrate-dependent.

[2] Integer-State Dynamics of Quantized Spiking Neural Networks for Efficient Hardware Acceleration

Lei Zhang

🧩 TL;DR

该研究从整数状态动力学视角分析硬件导向的脉冲神经网络，将SNN建模为有界整数格上的确定性映射，揭示了数值精度作为系统动力学设计变量的重要性，为硬件感知的SNN协同设计提供了分析框架。

📘 Detailed Summary

Motivation: 在脉冲神经网络的实际硬件实现中，膜电位、突触权重和阈值通常采用有限精度整数算术表示，量化、截断和溢出不仅会近似高精度模型，更会改变网络动力学行为，而现有研究缺乏对这一整数状态系统内在特性的深入分析。

Method: 研究采用整数状态动力学视角，将硬件导向的SNN建模为有界整数格上的确定性映射，引入具有整数值状态和基于移位的泄漏机制的轻量级更新规则，并通过探索性仿真验证方法，网络规模N=30-130，连接密度0.1-0.9，位宽4/8/16，时间步长T=1000。

Result: 实验结果显示系统表现出有界且循环的时间结构，对量化具有高度敏感性，观察到的动力学状态严重依赖于表示语义和缩放选择，表明数值精度显著影响网络行为模式。

Conclusion: 数值精度应被视为动力学设计变量而非简单近似误差，整数状态分析为硬件感知的SNN协同设计提供了有用框架，未来研究方向包括吸引子分析、精度感知训练以及FPGA/ASIC硬件验证。

📄 Abstract

Spiking neural networks (SNNs) support energy-efficient machine intelligence because event-driven computation and sparse activity map naturally to low-power digital hardware. In practical implementations, however, membrane states, synaptic weights, and thresholds are represented with finite-precision integer arithmetic. Quantization, clipping, and overflow can therefore alter network dynamics, not just approximate a higher-precision model. This paper adopts an integer-state dynamical perspective, modeling a hardware-oriented SNN as a deterministic map on a bounded integer lattice. Under this view, recurrence, periodic orbits, and regime changes become intrinsic properties of the system. We introduce a lightweight update rule with integer-valued states and shift-based leakage, and demonstrate the approach through exploratory simulations with network sizes N = 30-130, connection densities 0.1-0.9, and bit widths 4/8/16 over T = 1000 steps. The results show bounded and recurrent temporal structure with strong quantization sensitivity. The observed regimes depend heavily on representation semantics and scaling choices. These findings suggest that numerical precision acts as a dynamical design variable and highlight integer-state analysis as a useful framework for hardware-aware SNN co-design, motivating future work on attractor analysis, precision-aware training, and FPGA/ASIC validation.
