### [1] [CMTFormer: Marrying Transformer with Hierarchical Information Interaction for RGB-Event Object Detection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29136)
*Yu Li, Yuenan Hou, Yingmei Wei, Jiangming Chen, Yanming Guo*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
提出CMTFormer，一种层级式交叉模态信息交互Transformer，通过浅层对齐模块（SAM）、中层交叉增强模块（CEM）和深层可学习融合模块（LDFM）分阶段融合RGB与事件流信息，在DSEC-Detection和PKU-DAVIS-SOD数据集上取得领先性能。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 事件相机具有高时间分辨率和动态范围，但RGB帧与事件流之间存在固有的异构性，先前多模态融合方法容易在跨模态融合过程中导致噪声放大或冗余特征集成，难以实现高效稳定的多模态协作。

**Method:** 提出CMTFormer（Cross-Modal information inTeraction transFormer），采用从浅到深的分层级信息交互方案：浅层通过浅层对齐模块（SAM）融合RGB与事件低层特征以缓解属性差异并抑制噪声；中层通过交叉增强模块（CEM）利用纹理和边缘信息生成相互增强的中层特征；深层通过可学习深度融合模块（LDFM）以可学习权重自适应聚合高层RGB与事件线索；此外还设计了空间先验模块（Spatial Prior Module）利用全局空间信息提升定位精度。

**Result:** 在两个主流基于事件的目标检测基准DSEC-Detection和PKU-DAVIS-SOD上进行大量实验，CMTFormer在单模态和多模态设置下均持续超越现有检测方法，充分证明了其范式的有效性。

**Conclusion:** 提出了一种分层级的跨模态融合范式，通过分阶段处理不同层次特征有效解决了RGB与事件流之间的异构性问题，为事件相机与传统RGB相机的多模态融合提供了稳健且高效的解决方案，代码将在论文发表后公开。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Event cameras capture sparse brightness changes with high temporal resolution and high dynamic range, compensating for the deficiencies of the conventional RGB frames. However, previous multi-modal fusion techniques typically fail to handle the inherent heterogeneity between RGB frames and event streams, thus easily leading to noise amplification or redundant feature integration during cross-modal fusion. In this paper, we propose a Cross-Modal information inTeraction transFormer, coined as CMTFormer, which hierarchically integrates RGB and event information to achieve efficient and stable multimodal collaboration. Specifically, we design a shallow-to-deep information interaction scheme. In the shallow stage, we present the Shallow Alignment Module (SAM) to achieve an efficient fusion of RGB and event low-level features, which mitigates attribute disparities and prevents noisy information. In the middle stage, we devise the Cross-modal Enhancement Module (CEM) that utilizes texture and edge information to produce mutually reinforced middle-level features. In the deep stage, we present the Learnable Deep Fusion Module (LDFM) which performs high-level information aggregation through learnable weights, thus enabling the network to adaptively fuse RGB and event clues. A Spatial Prior Module is further designed to utilize global spatial information to enhance localization accuracy. Extensive experiments are conducted on two prevalent event-based object detection benchmarks, i.e., DSEC-Detection and PKU-DAVIS-SOD. Our CMTFormer consistently surpasses the detection counterparts in both uni-modal and multi-modal settings, strongly demonstrating the effectiveness of our paradigm. Codes will be available upon publication.


### [2] [EvLIR: Learning Illumination Residuals from Ordered Events for Low-Light Image Enhancement](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29430)
*Haoxian Zhou, Chuanzhi Xu, Langyi Chen, Pengfei Ye, Haodong Chen, Qiang Qu, Ali Anaissi, Weidong Cai*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
提出EvLIR框架，利用有序事件时间箱和时序残差模块（ConvGRU）建模事件动态，生成光照校正引导Retinex风格的低光图像增强，在四个基准上十一项指标中取得最优。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 低光图像增强在输入存在结构缺失、饱和噪声和弱局部对比时严重不适定；事件相机虽能提供高时间分辨率亮度变化观测，但现有工作通常将事件体素通道视为无序或静态特征堆叠，忽略了时间箱内的时序演化信息，削弱了事件数据的有用时序证据。

**Method:** 提出EvLIR框架，保留事件流的有序时间箱结构，引入轻量级ConvGRU构成的时序事件残差模块（TERM）编码短窗口事件动态，将所得时序状态转换为有界光照校正，为Retinex风格光照估计提供空间自适应光度引导，并最终进行可靠性感知的图像-事件联合恢复。

**Result:** 在SDE和SDSD室内/室外四个基准上，EvLIR在十二项数据集-指标对中取得十一项最优，平均PSNR达25.63 dB、PSNR*达28.30 dB、SSIM达0.827。

**Conclusion:** 显式建模事件时间箱内的时序演化能有效提升低光图像增强性能，时序残差建模与Retinex光照估计相结合是一种有前景的事件-图像融合范式，为利用事件相机时序优势解决不适定图像恢复问题提供了新思路。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Low-light image enhancement is severely ill-posed when the input frame contains missing structure, saturated noise, and weak local contrast. Event cameras provide asynchronous brightness-change observations with high temporal resolution, but prior works often treat voxel channels as an unordered or static feature stack before fusion, rather than explicitly modeling their within-window temporal evolution, weakening the temporal evidence that makes events useful. We propose EvLIR, a temporal-residual enhancement framework that learns illumination residuals from ordered events for low-light image enhancement. Given a low-light frame and its aligned event voxel, EvLIR preserves the ordered temporal bins of the event stream and introduces a Temporal Event Residual Module (TERM) to encode short-window event dynamics with a lightweight ConvGRU. The resulting temporal state is converted into a bounded illumination correction, which provides spatially adaptive photometric guidance for Retinex-style illumination estimation and subsequent reliability-aware image-event restoration. On SDE and SDSD indoor/outdoor benchmarks, EvLIR achieves the best result on eleven of twelve dataset-metric pairs, with average scores of 25.63~dB PSNR, 28.30~dB PSNR*, and 0.827 SSIM across the four benchmarks.

### [3] [Unified Complex-valued Neural Network: A Magnitude-Phase Computational Model for Event-Driven Neuromorphic Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29099)
*Reza Ahmadvand, Sarah Safura Sharif, Yaser Mike Banad*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出基于统一复数神经元（UCN）的新型神经网络模型，通过复数状态统一连续值编码与脉冲时序事件生成，并结合反向传播和事件驱动自适应相位学习实现高效时空学习，在目标跟踪和洛伦兹吸引子学习任务中验证了其准确性与可解释性。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 传统ANN擅长连续值表示，SNN擅长事件驱动时序处理，但两者均无法在单一计算结构中同时学习数值编码与时序动态特性，缺乏统一的理论框架和训练方法。

**Method:** 提出统一复数神经元（UCN），其复数状态中幅度编码信号强度，相位控制时间演化与脉冲发射；构建BP与BPTT结合的联合训练框架优化幅度与相位通路，进而提出事件驱动自适应相位学习（EAPL）规则作为低复杂度替代方案。

**Result:** 在目标跟踪和洛伦兹吸引子学习任务上，UCN网络（UCNN）实现了准确、稳定且可解释的时空学习，同时保持稀疏事件驱动计算特性，适用于神经形态与边缘AI应用。

**Conclusion:** UCN通过复数状态统一了连续值编码与脉冲时序生成两种计算范式，为构建兼具表示精度与事件驱动效率的神经网络提供了新途径，其轻量化训练规则进一步提升了在资源受限场景下的实用潜力。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Artificial neural networks (ANN) provide accurate continuous-valued representation, whereas spiking neural networks (SNN) offer event-driven temporal processing, yet both paradigms face limitations when value encoding and timing dynamics must be learned within a single computational structure. This paper introduces a network based on Unified Complex-valued Neuron (UCN), a new neural computational model that integrates continuous activation and phase-driven event generation through an asymmetric complex-valued state. In the UCN, magnitude encodes signal strength while phase governs intrinsic temporal evolution and valued spike emission. A foundational training framework combining backpropagation (BP) and backpropagation through time (BPTT) is first developed to optimize magnitude and phase pathways in a unified way. To reduce computational complexity, an event-driven adaptive phase learning (EAPL) rule is then introduced as a more efficient alternative. The proposed model is evaluated through object tracking and Lorenz attractor learning. Results demonstrate that UCN-based Network (UCNN) provides accurate, stable, and interpretable spatiotemporal learning while preserving sparse event-driven computation for neuromorphic and edge-AI applications.
