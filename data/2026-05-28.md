### [1] [Automated Estimation of Impact Time, Impact Location, and Shuttlecock Speed in Badminton Smashes Using Event Cameras](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28011)
*Yudai Washida, Yuto Kase, Kai Ishibe, Ryoma Yasuda, Sakiko Hashimoto*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出一种使用双同步事件相机的方法，可在同一试次中自动集成估计羽毛球杀球的击球时间、球拍面击球位置和击球后球速，通过Bland-Altman分析验证了其与高速相机基准方法的一致性，表明该方法可作为实用场景下羽毛球杀球表现与装备评估的有效工具。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 量化羽毛球杀球中的冲击现象对评估运动表现和装备性能至关重要，但传统测量系统在时间分辨率、数据效率和准备工作量之间存在权衡，缺乏一种能够同时集成估计击球时间、击球位置和球速的高效方法。

**Method:** 提出一种使用两台同步事件相机的方法：从事件率统计检测挥拍区间，从侧视视角事件数据中羽毛球轨迹拐点估计击球时间，从后视视角事件图像中对球拍面进行椭圆拟合确定击球位置，并在矢状面计算击球后球速。使用125次杀球试次（5名运动员）进行验证，以高速相机为基准进行Bland-Altman分析。

**Result:** 在124个可分析试次中成功估计了击球时间和球速，93.5%（116/124）的试次成功估计了击球位置。击球时间、内外侧击球位置、纵向击球位置和球速的偏差（95%置信区间）分别为1.84 ms（1.45至2.23）、3.45 mm（2.18至4.72）、-1.92 mm（-2.97至-0.88）和-1.00 m/s（-2.46至0.46），所有指标均未观察到比例偏差。

**Conclusion:** 该方法能够在实际场景中实现对羽毛球杀球表现和装备的集成评估，具有良好的准确性和实用性，为运动科学和装备测试提供了高效的新工具。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Quantifying impact phenomena in badminton smashes is important for evaluating both athletic performance and equipment; however, conventional measurement systems involve trade-offs between temporal resolution, data efficiency, and preparation effort. This study proposes a measurement method using two synchronized event cameras to automatically estimate impact time, impact location on the racket face, and post-impact shuttlecock speed in an integrated manner within the same trial. The swing interval was detected from event rate statistics, impact time was estimated from the shuttlecock trajectory inflection in the lateral-view event data, impact location was determined by ellipse fitting to the racket face in the rear-view event image, and shuttlecock speed was calculated in the sagittal plane. To validate the proposed method, Bland-Altman analysis was performed against a high-speed camera-based reference method using 125 smash trials from five players. Impact time and shuttlecock speed were estimated in all 124 analyzable trials, and impact location was estimated in 93.5% (116/124). The bias (95% CI) for impact time, medio-lateral impact location, longitudinal impact location, and shuttlecock speed were 1.84 ms (1.45 to 2.23), 3.45 mm (2.18 to 4.72), -1.92 mm (-2.97 to -0.88), and -1.00 m/s (-2.46 to 0.46), respectively. No proportional bias was observed for any metric. These results suggest that the proposed method can serve as a useful tool for integrated assessment of badminton smash performance and equipment in practical settings.


### [2] [Beyond Surrogate Gradients: Fully Differentiable Token Pruning for Vision-Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28051)
*Landi He, Mingde Yao, Shawn Young, Lijian Xu*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
DiffPrune 提出将视觉token剪枝重新建模为连续信息控制问题，通过基于重要性分数的方差保持噪声调节每个token，替代传统的离散选择近似学习，在保留96.5%全模型精度的同时实现2.85倍LLM预填充加速。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 现有视觉token剪枝方法通常依赖Gumbel-Softmax对离散选择进行近似训练，但其优化由替代梯度驱动而非真实选择过程，导致token重要性的学习不可靠，需要一种完全可微的剪枝机制来提升训练质量。

**Method:** DiffPrune 提出Information Throttler机制，通过基于重要性分数的方差保持噪声调节每个token的信息量：在训练阶段，高重要性分数对应较少的信息抑制，从而实现完全可微的重要性学习路径；推理阶段则对学习到的分数进行硬阈值化，直接移除低重要性token。

**Result:** 在十个VLM基准测试上，DiffPrune保留了全模型96.5%的准确率，将LLM预填充速度提升2.85倍，且推理开销仅为0.69毫秒。

**Conclusion:** DiffPrune通过将token剪枝从离散选择学习重新定义为连续信息控制，提供了一条完全可微的优化路径，有效提升了重要性学习的可靠性，在计算效率与模型性能之间取得了优异的平衡。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Visual token pruning reduces the computational cost of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) by removing redundant visual tokens. Existing methods typically rely on Gumbel-Softmax to approximate discrete selection during training. However, the optimization is driven by surrogate gradients rather than the true selection process, leading to unreliable learning of token importance. In this paper, we propose DiffPrune, which reformulates pruning as continuous control of token information instead of discrete selection learning. Specifically, we introduce an Information Throttler that modulates each token using variance-preserving noise conditioned on importance scores, where higher scores induce less information suppression during training. This design directly operates on token representations, naturally providing a fully differentiable optimization path for learning token importance. At inference, tokens are removed via hard thresholding on the learned scores. Across ten VLM benchmarks, DiffPrune retains 96.5% of full-model accuracy while accelerating LLM prefill by 2.85x, with only 0.69 ms of inference overhead.

### [3] [Benchmarking Fairness in Spiking Neural Networks: Data Bias, Spurious Features, and Hardware Effects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27407)
*Hudi He, Fukun Wang, Zhe Wang, Xinyi Wang, Shuhan Ye, Jiarui Liu, Qing Qing, Ziqi Xu, Xikun Zhang, Renqiang Luo*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文首次为脉冲神经网络（SNN）建立了系统性公平性基准，涵盖训练数据人口覆盖缺口、虚假特征泄漏以及部署环境失配三个现实维度，并集成了跨人口数据集、受控偏差注入和神经形态硬件模拟器，揭示了现有SNN在不同群体和资源约束下存在的显著公平性差距。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 现有SNN公平性评估受限于浅层数据集多样性和理想化硬件假设，缺乏能够反映现实世界复杂性的系统性基准，无法准确衡量SNN在人口覆盖、虚假特征关联以及边缘部署环境失配等关键维度上的公平性表现。

**Method:** 本文提出了首个系统性SNN公平性基准框架，包含三个关键维度：训练数据的人口覆盖缺口、虚假特征泄漏（如肤色作为类别标签的代理变量）以及部署环境失配（如边缘设备受限的脉冲编码）。框架集成了四个跨人口数据集、受控偏差注入机制以及三种神经形态硬件模拟器（Loihi 2, SpiNNaker），支持在资源约束下对公平性-性能权衡进行隔离分析。

**Result:** 对12种最先进SNN的标准化评估显示：基于有偏数据训练的模型对少数群体的假阳性率高出23%；硬件限制如脉冲精度降低在边缘部署中进一步将准确率差距扩大至41%；此外，为云端SNN设计的偏差缓解策略在资源受限条件下往往性能下降。

**Conclusion:** 本文研究表明，SNN公平性研究需要联合优化公平性与硬件效率的协同设计原则，弥补了算法公平性研究与神经形态工程之间的鸿沟，为医疗、自主系统等社会关键应用中的可信SNN奠定了基础。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Evaluating fairness in Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) demands rigorous benchmarks that reflect real-world complexities, yet existing assessments remain limited by superficial dataset diversity and idealized hardware assumptions. This work introduces the first systematic fairness benchmark for SNNs, addressing three critical dimensions of realism: (1) demographic coverage gaps in training data, (2) spurious feature leakage (e.g., skin tone as a proxy for class labels), and (3) deployment-environment mismatches (e.g., edge devices with constrained spike encoding). Our framework integrates four cross-demographic datasets with controlled bias injections and three neuromorphic hardware simulators (Loihi 2, SpiNNaker), enabling isolated analysis of fairness-performance trade-offs under resource constraints. Standardized evaluations of 12 state-of-the-art SNNs reveal stark disparities: models trained on biased data exhibit 23\% higher false positive rates for underrepresented groups, while hardware limitations (e.g., reduced spike precision) further amplify accuracy gaps by up to 41\% in edge deployments. Critically, bias mitigation strategies developed for cloud-based SNNs often degrade under resource constraints, highlighting the need for co-design principles that jointly optimize fairness and hardware efficiency. By bridging algorithmic fairness research with neuromorphic engineering, our benchmark provides a foundation for trustworthy SNNs in socially critical applications such as healthcare and autonomous systems. Our code is available at: https://anonymous.4open.science/r/SNN-Benchmarks-8017.


### [4] [STARS: Spike Tail-Aware Relational Synthesis for ANN-to-SNN Data-Free Knowledge Distillation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27409)
*Shuhan Ye, Yi Yu, Qixin Zhang, Hui Lu, Jiaming He, Qinggang Zhang, Li Shen, Xudong Jiang*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
提出STARS方法，通过关系一致性对齐和尾部感知正则化两个互补目标增强标准BN引导的合成数据生成，在无原始训练数据的ANN-to-SNN知识蒸馏场景下，有效提升了SNN学生网络的性能，在多个数据集上取得了最高6.7%的提升。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** ANN-to-SNN知识蒸馏在原始训练数据不可用的实际部署场景中面临挑战，现有无数据知识蒸馏方法依赖教师侧先验（尤其是BN统计量）来合成替代数据，但这些面向ANN的约束主要正则化均值和方差，对于响应依赖于阈值穿越动力学的SNN学生网络而言约束不足，导致合成数据对SNN的信息量有限。

**Method:** 提出Spike Tail-Aware Relational Synthesis方法，在标准BN引导的合成数据生成基础上新增两个互补目标：关系一致性对齐，保持教师与学生之间跨样本的关系一致性；尾部感知正则化，通过教师导出阈值上的软超越概率来正则化与阈值相关的尾部概率，从而生成既保持教师有效性又对SNN学生更具信息量的合成批次数据。

**Result:** 在CIFAR-10、CIFAR-100和Tiny-ImageNet数据集上，跨多种ANN-SNN配对进行实验，STARS方法持续优于传统无数据知识蒸馏基线，甚至在CIFAR-10上提升达4.6%，在CIFAR-100上提升达6.7%，超越了若干需要原始数据的有数据知识蒸馏方法。

**Conclusion:** 在面向SNN的无数据知识蒸馏中，仅靠BN匹配是不够的，必须补充关系性和尾部感知约束来生成对SNN学生更有信息量的合成数据，STARS作为即插即用方法有效缩小了ANN与SNN之间的性能差距。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
SNNs promise energy-efficient and low-latency inference, but their performance still trails that of ANNs. ANN-to-SNN knowledge distillation helps narrow this gap, yet the original training data are often unavailable in practical deployment settings. Existing data-free knowledge distillation (DFKD) methods synthesize surrogate data by matching teacher-side priors, especially BN statistics, but these ANN-oriented constraints mainly regularize mean and variance and therefore remain under-constrained for SNN students whose responses depend on threshold-crossing dynamics. In this paper, we propose Spike Tail-Aware Relational Synthesis (STARS), a plug-and-play method for ANN-to-SNN DFKD that augments standard BN-guided synthesis with two complementary objectives: Relational Consistency Alignment, which preserves cross-sample relational consistency between teacher and student, and Tail-Aware Regularization, which regularizes threshold-relevant tail probabilities through soft exceedance over teacher-derived thresholds. Together, these objectives generate synthetic batches that remain teacher-valid while becoming more informative for SNN students. Experiments on CIFAR-10, CIFAR-100, and Tiny-ImageNet across multiple ANN-SNN pairs show that our method consistently improves conventional DFKD baselines and even surpasses several KD methods, with gains of up to 4.6\% on CIFAR-10 and 6.7\% on CIFAR-100, highlighting the importance of complementing BN matching with relational and tail-aware constraints in SNN-oriented DFKD.


### [5] [Advancing Direct Training for Spiking Neural Networks with Circulate-Firing Neurons and Learnable Gradients](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27412)
*Feifan Zhou, Xiang Wei, Yang Liu, Qiang Yu*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
提出一种新的SNN直接训练算法，包含循环发放神经元模型、可学习逐时间步代理梯度函数和正负平衡损失函数，有效提升SNN信息表示能力和梯度传播精度，在多个数据集和Transformer架构上取得优于现有方法的性能。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 脉冲神经网络（SNN）虽具有能效优势，但与人工神经网络（ANN）之间仍存在显著性能差距，其原因包括两个方面：传统脉冲神经元对膜电位的丰富动态利用不足导致信息表示能力有限，以及固定代理梯度函数在时间步上导致梯度传播不精确，阻碍了有效的直接训练。

**Method:** 提出一种新的SNN直接训练算法，包含三项核心创新：一是循环发放（circulate-firing）脉冲神经元模型，通过更有效地利用膜电位动态增强信息表示能力；二是逐时间步可学习代理梯度函数，在反向传播中实现精确梯度估计；三是正负平衡损失函数，通过平衡正负膜电位进一步促进性能提升。该方法可无缝推广至Transformer等先进架构。

**Result:** 在多个数据集上的广泛实验表明，所提方法取得了具有竞争力的性能，且在与Transformer架构结合时持续优于现有方法。

**Conclusion:** 该工作揭示了进一步利用SNN内在膜电位动态对于提升性能的有效性，为构建高性能脉冲神经架构开辟了新途径。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) have emerged with promising energy-efficient property, yet a substantial performance gap persists compared to Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs). This gap stems from at least two key limitations: first, conventional spiking neurons offer limited information representation capacity, underutilizing the rich dynamics of membrane potentials; second, fixed surrogate gradient (SG) functions across time steps leads to imprecise gradient propagation, impeding effective direct training. To address these two challenges, we propose a new direct training algorithm with three core innovations: first, a circulate-firing spiking neuron model that enhances information representation capacity by leveraging membrane potentials more effectively; second, a time-step-wise learnable surrogate gradient function, enabling accurate gradient estimation during backpropagation; third, a positive-negative balanced loss function to achieve equilibrium between positive and negative membrane potentials and further boost SNN performance. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our methods achieve competitive performance across multiple datasets. Our methods can generalize seamlessly to advanced architectures of Transformer, consistently outperforming existing methods. Our work highlights the effectiveness of further harnessing intrinsic membrane dynamics of SNNs for performance improvement, and thus open a new avenue for advancing high-performance spiking neural architectures.

### [6] [Modeling Vehicle-Type-Specific Pedestrian Crash Avoidance Behavior in Safety-Critical Interactions Using Smooth-Mamba Deep Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28552)
*Qingwen Pu, Kun Xie, Hong Yang, Di Yang, Junqing Wang*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出SMamba-DDPG框架，利用Argoverse 2数据集提取安全关键的行人-车辆交互事件，建模行人与自动驾驶车辆（AV）及人类驾驶车辆（HDV）的碰撞规避行为。实验表明该方法在行为再现、反应时间及冲突率分析上均优于基线模型，并揭示了行人对AV反应更快、过街速度更低，且AV场景下冲突率更低、让行率更高。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 随着自动驾驶车辆（AV）与人类驾驶车辆（HDV）在道路上共存，理解行人在安全关键交互中如何对不同车辆类型做出反应，对于自动驾驶技术的安全部署至关重要。现有研究缺乏对AV与HDV场景下行人碰撞规避行为的精细化、车辆类型特异性建模和量化比较。

**Method:** 从Argoverse 2数据集中提取安全关键的行人-车辆交互事件，提出SMamba-DDPG（Smooth-Mamba Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient）框架，融合平滑动作约束与高效时序表征学习（Mamba结构）。针对AV和HDV两种场景分别训练独立的碰撞规避策略，以量化行人行为差异。

**Result:** SMamba-DDPG在再现行人碰撞规避行为上优于强化学习与监督学习基线模型；重建轨迹在AV和HDV场景中均准确复现了碰撞规避运动学特征。反应时间分析显示模型捕捉到类人的响应延迟，且行人对AV的反应速度快于HDV；反事实分析表明行人与AV交互时采用更低的过街速度；大规模安全分析显示行人-AV交互的冲突率更低、行人让行率更高。

**Conclusion:** 研究结果表明行人对AV和HDV的行为存在显著差异，强调在混合交通环境中设计更安全的自动驾驶系统和更真实的交通仿真时，需引入车辆类型特异性的行人行为模型。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
As automated vehicles (AVs) increasingly share roadways with human-driven vehicles (HDVs), understanding how pedestrians respond to different vehicle types in safety-critical interactions is essential for the safe deployment of automated driving technologies. This study extracts safety-critical pedestrian-vehicle interactions from the Argoverse 2 dataset to capture real-world crash avoidance behaviors in encounters involving AVs and HDVs. To model vehicle-type-specific pedestrian crash avoidance behavior, we develop a Smooth-Mamba Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient framework, termed SMamba-DDPG, which integrates smooth action constraints with efficient temporal representation learning. To quantify pedestrian behavioral differences, the framework trains separate crash avoidance policies for pedestrian interactions with AVs and HDVs. Results show that SMamba-DDPG outperforms baseline reinforcement learning and supervised learning models in reproducing pedestrian crash avoidance behaviors. Reconstructed trajectories demonstrate strong behavioral realism, accurately reproducing crash avoidance kinematics in both AV and HDV scenarios. Reaction time analysis shows that the model captures human-like response delays and reveals that pedestrians respond more quickly to AVs than to HDVs. Counterfactual analysis further indicates that pedestrians adopt lower crossing speeds when interacting with AVs. Large-scale safety analysis of model-generated data revealed that pedestrian-AV interactions consistently yielded lower conflict rates and higher pedestrian yielding rates compared to pedestrian-HDV interactions. The findings highlight the importance of incorporating vehicle-type-specific pedestrian behavioral models for safer automated driving system design and more realistic traffic simulations in mixed-traffic environments.
