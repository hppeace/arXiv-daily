### [1] [REALM: An RGB and Event Aligned Latent Manifold for Cross-Modal Perception](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00271)
*Vincenzo Polizzi, David B. Lindell, Jonathan Kelly*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
REALM是一个跨模态框架，通过LoRA将事件相机数据对齐到预训练的RGB ViT基础模型潜空间中，无需任务特定训练即可实现深度估计、语义分割和零样本宽基线特征匹配，在特征匹配任务上达到SOTA。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 事件相机具有高时间分辨率、低延迟和极端光照鲁棒性等优势，但现有基于学习的处理方法通常局限于狭窄的任务特定场景，缺乏跨模态泛化能力，无法利用大规模RGB预训练模型中蕴含的丰富几何和语义先验。

**Method:** 提出REALM框架，通过学习RGB与事件对齐的潜空间，将事件表示投影到预训练的RGB基础模型（ViT架构）的潜空间中；采用低秩适配（LoRA）桥接模态差距，冻结RGB骨干网络以保留其先验知识；下游任务（深度估计、语义分割）仅需将从RGB教师模型迁移的线性头即可完成，且支持将图像训练的复杂解码器（如MASt3R）零样本应用于原始事件数据。

**Result:** REALM在宽基线特征匹配任务上显著超越专门的专用架构，取得最先进性能；通过简单迁移RGB教师模型的线性头即可在深度估计和语义分割等下游任务上有效运作；成功实现了将图像预训练的复杂解码器零样本直接应用于事件数据。

**Conclusion:** 研究表明，通过模态对齐和LoRA微调可以有效利用冻结的RGB基础模型处理异步事件流，打破了事件处理的任务特定限制；该框架为跨模态事件视觉提供了一种通用、可扩展的范式，尤其在高要求的几何匹配任务中展现出巨大潜力。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Event cameras provide several unique advantages over standard frame-based sensors, including high temporal resolution, low latency, and robustness to extreme lighting. However, existing learning-based approaches for event processing are typically confined to narrow, task-specific silos and lack the ability to generalize across modalities. We address this gap with REALM, a cross-modal framework that learns an RGB and Event Aligned Latent Manifold by projecting event representations into the pretrained latent space of RGB foundation models. Instead of task-specific training, we leverage low-rank adaptation (LoRA) to bridge the modality gap, effectively unlocking the geometric and semantic priors of frozen RGB backbones for asynchronous event streams. We demonstrate that REALM effectively maps events into the ViT-based foundation latent space. Our method allows us to perform downstream tasks like depth estimation and semantic segmentation by simply transferring linear heads trained on the RGB teacher. Most significantly, REALM enables the direct, zero-shot application of complex, frozen image-trained decoders, such as MASt3R, to raw event data. We demonstrate state-of-the-art performance in wide-baseline feature matching, significantly outperforming specialized architectures. Code and models are available upon acceptance.


### [2] [Real-Time Frame- and Event-based Object Detection with Spiking Neural Networks on Edge Neuromorphic Hardware: Design, Deployment and Benchmark](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00146)
*Udayanga G. W. K. N. Gamage, Yan Zeng, Cesar Cadena, Matteo Fumagalli, Silvia Tolu*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了一套面向神经形态处理器Intel Loihi 2的脉冲神经网络（SNN）目标检测架构设计方法，并采用ANN-to-SNN蒸馏感知训练，在实现实时检测的同时取得了所有平台中最低的单次推理动态能耗，且与ANN相比恢复了87-100%的检测精度。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 在无人机巡检、自主导航和移动机器人等能量受限平台上，实时目标检测对能效要求极高；尽管脉冲神经网络（SNN）在神经形态硬件上被认为比传统人工神经网络（ANN）更节能，但针对神经形态处理器设计通用SNN检测架构并实现工程部署的系统性方法仍然缺乏。

**Method:** 本文提出了一套面向神经形态平台的通用SNN检测架构设计方法，并给出了在最新一代神经形态处理器Intel Loihi 2上部署所需的工程适配；同时引入了ANN-to-SNN蒸馏感知训练（distillation-aware training），使SNN能够从预训练的ANN教师模型中蒸馏知识，以弥补检测精度损失。

**Result:** 在Loihi 2上基于帧和事件两种数据集进行目标检测实验，与NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano、Jetson Nano B01和Apple M2 CPU上的ANN检测对比：SNN在Loihi 2上实现了实时检测，且每推理动态能耗最低，功耗也优于其他平台；ANN在Jetson Orin Nano上推理速率更高。蒸馏感知训练使SNN恢复了ANN检测精度的87-100%，且推理延迟更低，而未蒸馏的SNN精度下降了11-27%。

**Conclusion:** 本研究表明神经形态系统在边缘端能效优先的实时目标检测任务中具有显著潜力，Loihi 2在动态能耗和功耗方面优于传统嵌入式AI平台，结合蒸馏训练可大幅缩小SNN与ANN之间的检测精度差距，为低功耗边缘检测提供了一条可行路径。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Real-time object detection on energy-constrained platforms is critical for applications such as UAV-based inspection, autonomous navigation, and mobile robotics. Spiking neural networks (SNNs) on neuromorphic hardware are believed to be significantly more energy-efficient than conventional artificial neural networks (ANNs). In this work, we present a comprehensive methodology for designing general SNN detection architectures targeting neuromorphic platforms, along with the engineering adaptations required to deploy them on the state-of-the-art Neuromorphic processor, Intel Loihi 2. We benchmark SNN-based object detection on Loihi 2 using both frame-based and event-based datasets, comparing performance with ANN-based detection on the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, NVIDIA Jetson Nano B01, and the Apple M2 CPU. Our results show that SNNs on Loihi 2 can perform real-time detection while achieving the lowest per-inference dynamic energy among all platforms. Also, Loihi 2 outperforms the other platforms in terms of power consumption, though ANNs on Jetson Orin Nano achieve higher inference rates. Furthermore, our ANN-to-SNN distillation-aware training enables SNNs to recover 87-100% of the detection accuracy of their ANN counterparts while maintaining lower inference latency; without distillation, SNNs exhibit an 11-27% accuracy drop. These results highlight the potential of neuromorphic systems for energy-efficient, real-time object detection at the edge.

### [3] [Scalable Learning in Structured Recurrent Spiking Neural Networks without Backpropagation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00402)
*Bo Tang, Weiwei Xie*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
提出了一种结合局部密集递归层与稀疏小世界长程投射的结构化多层递归SNN架构，并引入基于种群WTA教学信号、固定随机对齐反馈路径和低维调质神经元群体的生物启发式学习框架，通过纯局部可塑性机制实现监督学习，无需反向传播或代理梯度，在基准分类任务上展示了稳定的学习性能与硬件兼容性。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 深度递归脉冲神经网络在可扩展学习上面临重大挑战，尤其是稀疏连接架构下缺乏有效的监督学习方法，现有基于梯度的方法（如反向传播和代理梯度）在生物合理性和硬件可扩展性方面存在局限，亟需一种无需梯度传播、支持局部突触更新且兼容稀疏通信的学习框架。

**Method:** 提出了一种结构化多层递归SNN架构，包含局部密集的递归层和通向读出种群的稀疏小世界长程投射（后者大部分连接固定以保持路由效率和硬件可扩展性）；引入纯局部可塑性机制的生物启发式监督学习框架，结合了输出层的种群WTA教学信号、固定的随机广播对齐反馈通路以及低维调质神经元群体，通过三因子学习规则和资格迹来门控突触更新。

**Result:** 对所提方法进行了算法性质、计算复杂度和硬件可行性分析，并在基准分类任务上证明了稳定的学习能力和具有竞争力的性能，表明结构化递归与神经调质学习能够使基于梯度的SNN训练以外的方法实现可扩展且硬件兼容的学习。

**Conclusion:** 该研究揭示了结构化递归连接与神经调质学习机制相结合可以有效替代传统梯度方法用于深度SNN训练，在保持生物合理性的同时具备硬件可扩展性，为面向能效计算和神经形态硬件的脉冲神经网络学习范式提供了新的方向。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) provide a promising framework for energy-efficient and biologically grounded computation; however, scalable learning in deep recurrent architectures with sparse connectivity remains a major challenge. In this work, we propose a structured multi-layer recurrent SNN architecture composed of locally dense recurrent layers augmented with sparse small-world long-range projections to a readout population. The long-range connectivity is largely fixed, preserving routing efficiency and hardware scalability, while synaptic adaptation is performed using strictly local plasticity mechanisms. To enable supervised learning without backpropagation or surrogate gradients, we introduce a biologically motivated learning framework that combines: (i) population-based winner-take-all (WTA) teaching signals at the output layer, (ii) fixed random broadcast alignment feedback pathways, and (iii) low-dimensional modulatory neuron populations that gate synaptic updates through three-factor learning rules with eligibility traces. This design supports deep recurrent computation with sparse global communication and purely local synaptic updates. We analyze the algorithmic properties, computational complexity, and hardware feasibility of the proposed approach, and demonstrate stable learning and competitive performance on benchmark classification tasks. The results highlight the potential of structured recurrence and neuromodulatory learning to enable scalable, hardware-compatible SNN training beyond gradient-based methods.
