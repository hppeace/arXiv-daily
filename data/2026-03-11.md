### [1] [HelixTrack: Event-Based Tracking and RPM Estimation of Propeller-like Objects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09235)
*Radim Spetlik, Michal Pliska, Vojtěch Vrba, Jiri Matas*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
HelixTrack提出了一种完全事件驱动的视觉跟踪方法，专门用于在无人机和旋转机械中实现微秒级延迟的螺旋桨状物体跟踪与转速估计，解决了传统基于帧和事件的方法在周期性运动下的失效问题。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 当前基于帧和事件的传统跟踪方法在无人机螺旋桨等快速周期性运动物体上存在漂移或失效问题，因为周期性特征违反了它们的平滑运动假设，而现有公开数据集缺乏针对螺旋桨状物体联合跟踪与转速估计的基准。

**Method:** HelixTrack采用完全事件驱动的方法，通过实时估计单应性矩阵将事件从图像平面反向映射到转子平面，利用卡尔曼滤波器维护瞬时相位估计，并通过批量迭代更新将相位残差与几何耦合来细化物体姿态，实现联合跟踪与转速估计。

**Result:** 在提出的Timestamped Quadcopter with Egomotion数据集上，HelixTrack能够以约11.8倍实时速度处理全速率事件，实现微秒级延迟，并在联合跟踪与转速估计任务上持续优于为转速估计调整的逐事件和聚合基线方法。

**Conclusion:** 该研究证明了完全事件驱动方法在快速周期性运动跟踪中的有效性，为解决无人机和旋转机械中的安全关键感知问题提供了新途径，同时发布的TQE数据集填补了该领域基准数据的空白。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Safety-critical perception for unmanned aerial vehicles and rotating machinery requires microsecond-latency tracking of fast, periodic motion under egomotion and strong distractors. Frame-based and event-based trackers drift or break on propellers because periodic signatures violate their smooth-motion assumptions. We tackle this gap with HelixTrack, a fully event-driven method that jointly tracks propeller-like objects and estimates their rotations per minute (RPM). Incoming events are back-warped from the image plane into the rotor plane via a homography estimated on the fly. A Kalman Filter maintains instantaneous estimates of phase. Batched iterative updates refine the object pose by coupling phase residuals to geometry. To our knowledge, no public dataset targets joint tracking and RPM estimation of propeller-like objects. We therefore introduce the Timestamped Quadcopter with Egomotion (TQE) dataset with 13 high-resolution event sequences, containing 52 rotating objects in total, captured at distances of 2 m / 4 m, with increasing egomotion and microsecond RPM ground truth. On TQE, HelixTrack processes full-rate events (approx. 11.8x real time) faster than real time and microsecond latency. It consistently outperforms per-event and aggregation-based baselines adapted for RPM estimation.


### [2] [EventVGGT: Exploring Cross-Modal Distillation for Consistent Event-based Depth Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09385)
*Yinrui Ren, Jinjing Zhu, Kanghao Chen, Zhuoxiao Li, Jing Ou, Zidong Cao, Tongyan Hua, Peilun Shi, Yingchun Fu, Wufan Zhao, Hui Xiong*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出EventVGGT框架，首次将视觉几何基础变换器（VGGT）的时空和多视图几何先验知识蒸馏到事件相机领域，通过建模事件流为连贯视频序列，显著提升了事件单目深度估计的准确性和时间一致性。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 事件相机在高速运动和极端光照条件下具有优越感知能力，但事件单目深度估计面临密集深度标注稀缺的挑战。现有无标注方法将事件流处理为独立帧，忽视了事件数据固有的时间连续性，无法利用视觉基础模型中丰富的时序先验，导致深度预测时间不一致且准确性不足。

**Method:** 提出EventVGGT框架，将事件流建模为连贯视频序列，首次从视觉几何基础变换器（VGGT）向事件域蒸馏时空和多视图几何先验。采用三级蒸馏策略：跨模态特征混合（CMFM）在输出层融合RGB和事件特征生成辅助深度预测；时空特征蒸馏（STFD）在特征层蒸馏VGGT的时空表示；时间一致性蒸馏（TCD）在时序层通过对齐帧间深度变化强制跨帧一致性。

**Result:** 在EventScape数据集上，EventVGGT将30米处的绝对平均深度误差降低了53%以上（从2.30降至1.06），显著优于现有方法。在未见过的DENSE和MVSEC数据集上展现出强大的零样本泛化能力，证明了框架的有效性和鲁棒性。

**Conclusion:** 该研究证明了将事件流建模为视频序列并蒸馏视觉基础模型的时空先验对提升深度估计性能的重要性。EventVGGT框架为事件相机深度估计提供了新范式，其三级蒸馏策略有效解决了模态差异和时间一致性问题，为事件视觉任务与基础模型结合开辟了新方向。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Event cameras offer superior sensitivity to high-speed motion and extreme lighting, making event-based monocular depth estimation a promising approach for robust 3D perception in challenging conditions. However, progress is severely hindered by the scarcity of dense depth annotations. While recent annotation-free approaches mitigate this by distilling knowledge from Vision Foundation Models (VFMs), a critical limitation persists: they process event streams as independent frames. By neglecting the inherent temporal continuity of event data, these methods fail to leverage the rich temporal priors encoded in VFMs, ultimately yielding temporally inconsistent and less accurate depth predictions. To address this, we introduce EventVGGT, a novel framework that explicitly models the event stream as a coherent video sequence. To the best of our knowledge, we are the first to distill spatio-temporal and multi-view geometric priors from the Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer (VGGT) into the event domain. We achieve this via a comprehensive tri-level distillation strategy: (i) Cross-Modal Feature Mixture (CMFM) bridges the modality gap at the output level by fusing RGB and event features to generate auxiliary depth predictions; (ii) Spatio-Temporal Feature Distillation (STFD) distills VGGT's powerful spatio-temporal representations at the feature level; and (iii) Temporal Consistency Distillation (TCD) enforces cross-frame coherence at the temporal level by aligning inter-frame depth changes. Extensive experiments demonstrate that EventVGGT consistently outperforms existing methods -- reducing the absolute mean depth error at 30m by over 53\% on EventScape (from 2.30 to 1.06) -- while exhibiting robust zero-shot generalization on the unseen DENSE and MVSEC datasets.

### [3] [Memory-Augmented Spiking Networks: Synergistic Integration of Complementary Mechanisms for Neuromorphic Vision](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08730)
*Effiong Blessing, Chiung-Yi Tseng, Isaac Nkrumah, Junaid Rehman*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本研究通过系统性的消融实验，探究了脉冲神经网络中多种记忆增强策略的集成效果，发现架构平衡而非单一优化能实现最佳性能，为神经形态系统的设计提供了原则性指导。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 尽管脉冲神经网络具有生物合理性和能效优势，但针对记忆增强策略的系统性研究仍然有限，本研究旨在填补这一空白，探索不同记忆增强组件在SNN中的集成效果与权衡关系。

**Method:** 研究采用五模型消融实验设计，在N-MNIST数据集上整合了泄漏积分发放神经元、监督对比学习、Hopfield网络和分层门控循环网络，系统评估了不同记忆增强策略的单独与组合效果。

**Result:** 基线SNN展现出结构化神经元群组，轮廓系数为0.687±0.012；监督对比学习提升准确率0.28%但降低聚类质量，HGRN在准确率和计算效率上均有显著提升；完全集成方案实现平衡改进，达到轮廓系数0.715±0.008、分类准确率97.49±0.10%、能耗1.85±0.06μJ和稀疏度97.0%。

**Conclusion:** 研究表明最优性能源于架构平衡而非孤立优化，监督对比学习与HGRN等组件存在互补性，这些发现为设计高效记忆增强型神经形态系统确立了基本原则，强调了整体架构协调的重要性。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) provide biological plausibility and energy efficiency, yet systematic investigations of memory augmentation strategies remain limited. We conduct a five-model ablation study integrating Leaky Integrate-and-Fire neurons, Supervised Contrastive Learning (SCL), Hopfield networks, and Hierarchical Gated Recurrent Networks (HGRN) on the N-MNIST dataset. Baseline SNNs exhibit organized neuronal groupings, or structured assemblies, characterized by a silhouette score of $0.687 \pm 0.012$. Individual augmentations introduce trade-offs: SCL improves accuracy by $0.28\%$ but reduces clustering (silhouette score $0.637 \pm 0.015$), while HGRN yields consistent gains in both accuracy ($+1.01\%$) and computational efficiency ($170.6\times$). Full integration achieves a balanced improvement across metrics, reaching a silhouette score of $0.715 \pm 0.008$, classification accuracy of $97.49 \pm 0.10\%$, energy consumption of $1.85 \pm 0.06\,μ\mathrm{J}$, and sparsity of $97.0\%$. These results indicate that optimal performance emerges from architectural balance rather than isolated optimization, establishing design principles for memory-augmented neuromorphic systems.
