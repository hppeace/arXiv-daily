### [1] [Event-based Photometric Stereo via Rotating Illumination and Per-Pixel Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10748)
*Hyunwoo Kim, Won-Hoe Kim, Sanghoon Lee, Jianfei Cai, Giljoo Nam, Jae-Sang Hyun*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了一种基于事件相机的光度立体视觉系统，通过单个移动光源和轻量级多层神经网络，无需系统标定即可从事件信号直接预测表面法线，在动态光照和高动态范围场景中表现出色。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 传统基于帧的光度立体视觉方法在实际应用中存在局限性，包括对可控光照条件的依赖以及对环境光照的敏感性，这限制了其在真实世界场景中的适用性。

**Method:** 该方法采用事件相机构建光度立体视觉系统，使用单个光源沿预定圆形轨迹移动，避免了多个同步光源的需求，并引入轻量级逐像素多层神经网络，直接从光源旋转产生的强度变化事件信号预测表面法线，无需系统标定。

**Result:** 在基准数据集和自主采集的真实世界数据上的实验结果表明，该方法相比现有事件基光度立体方法将平均角度误差降低了7.12%，在事件活动稀疏区域、强环境光照和镜面反射场景中表现出鲁棒性。

**Conclusion:** 该研究展示了事件相机在光度立体视觉中的优势，特别是在连续变化场景辐射和高动态范围条件下，提出的系统设计更紧凑、可扩展，且无需复杂标定过程，为实际应用提供了可行解决方案。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Photometric stereo is a technique for estimating surface normals using images captured under varying illumination. However, conventional frame-based photometric stereo methods are limited in real-world applications due to their reliance on controlled lighting, and susceptibility to ambient illumination. To address these limitations, we propose an event-based photometric stereo system that leverages an event camera, which is effective in scenarios with continuously varying scene radiance and high dynamic range conditions. Our setup employs a single light source moving along a predefined circular trajectory, eliminating the need for multiple synchronized light sources and enabling a more compact and scalable design. We further introduce a lightweight per-pixel multi-layer neural network that directly predicts surface normals from event signals generated by intensity changes as the light source rotates, without system calibration. Experimental results on benchmark datasets and real-world data collected with our data acquisition system demonstrate the effectiveness of our method, achieving a 7.12\% reduction in mean angular error compared to existing event-based photometric stereo methods. In addition, our method demonstrates robustness in regions with sparse event activity, strong ambient illumination, and scenes affected by specularities.

### [2] [Intrinsic Numerical Robustness and Fault Tolerance in a Neuromorphic Algorithm for Scientific Computing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10246)
*Bradley H. Theilman, James B. Aimone*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
该研究证明了一种先前描述的、用于求解偏微分方程的本征脉冲神经形态算法对神经元消融和脉冲丢失等结构扰动具有内在容错性，且这种鲁棒性可通过结构超参数进行调节。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 长期以来，神经形态计算被认为具有内在容错潜力，但大脑在神经形态应用中的鲁棒性尚未得到实证验证。本研究旨在探索神经形态算法是否能够展现出类似大脑的结构扰动容错能力。

**Method:** 研究采用了一种先前描述的、本征脉冲的神经形态算法来求解偏微分方程，并系统性地测试了该算法对神经元消融和脉冲丢失两种结构扰动的响应。通过调节结构超参数来探索鲁棒性的可调性。

**Result:** 实验结果表明，该算法对结构扰动具有显著的容错能力：在多达32%的神经元被消融或高达90%的脉冲被丢弃的情况下，算法精度仍无明显下降。这种鲁棒性可通过结构超参数进行调节，显示出可调的容错特性。

**Conclusion:** 这项工作首次实证证明了特定的大脑启发式算法能够实现显著的鲁棒性，验证了神经形态计算内在容错性的假设。研究结果表明，算法的大脑样灵感确实为其带来了预期的鲁棒特性，为开发具有容错能力的神经形态系统提供了重要依据。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
The potential for neuromorphic computing to provide intrinsic fault tolerance has long been speculated, but the brain's robustness in neuromorphic applications has yet to be demonstrated. Here, we show that a previously described, natively spiking neuromorphic algorithm for solving partial differential equations is intrinsically tolerant to structural perturbations in the form of ablated neurons and dropped spikes. The tolerance band for these perturbations is large: we find that as many as 32 percent of the neurons and up to 90 percent of the spikes may be entirely dropped before a significant degradation in the accuracy results. Furthermore, this robustness is tunable through structural hyperparameters. This work demonstrates that the specific brain-like inspiration behind the algorithm contributes to a significant degree of robustness expected from brain-like neuromorphic algorithms.


### [3] [An Event-Driven E-Skin System with Dynamic Binary Scanning and real time SNN Classification](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10537)
*Gaishan Li, Zhengnan Fu, Anubhab Tripathi, Junyi Yang, Arindam Basu*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了一种用于高速、事件稀疏采样电子皮肤的新型硬件系统，该系统集成了传感和神经形态计算，通过事件驱动的二进制扫描搜索策略和卷积脉冲神经网络实现了高效的手写数字识别。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 传统电子皮肤系统在数据采集和处理方面存在效率瓶颈，需要处理大量冗余数据，导致计算开销大、能耗高，限制了其在实时机器人感知和人机交互中的应用。

**Method:** 该系统围绕16x16压阻式触觉阵列构建，引入事件驱动的二进制扫描搜索策略进行数字分类，生成的稀疏数据流由在FPGA上实现的多层卷积脉冲神经网络处理，并构建了使用地址事件表示的神经形态触觉数据集。

**Result:** 该系统实现了扫描次数减少12.8倍、数据压缩率38.2倍、等效动态范围28.4倍以及99%的数据稀疏度，相比CNN仅需65%的计算量和15.6%的权重存储，同时保持92.11%的实时手写数字分类准确率。

**Conclusion:** 该研究展示了一个从模拟传感到神经形态分类的完全集成事件驱动管道，为机器人感知和人机交互提供了高效解决方案，证明了神经形态计算在触觉感知系统中的显著优势。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
This paper presents a novel hardware system for high-speed, event-sparse sampling-based electronic skin (e-skin)that integrates sensing and neuromorphic computing. The system is built around a 16x16 piezoresistive tactile array with front end and introduces a event-based binary scan search strategy to classify the digits. This event-driven strategy achieves a 12.8x reduction in scan counts, a 38.2x data compression rate and a 28.4x equivalent dynamic range, a 99% data sparsity, drastically reducing the data acquisition overhead. The resulting sparse data stream is processed by a multi-layer convolutional spiking neural network (Conv-SNN) implemented on an FPGA, which requires only 65% of the computation and 15.6% of the weight storage relative to a CNN. Despite these significant efficiency gains, the system maintains a high classification accuracy of 92.11% for real-time handwritten digit recognition. Furthermore, a real neuromorphic tactile dataset using Address Event Representation (AER) is constructed. This work demonstrates a fully integrated, event-driven pipeline from analog sensing to neuromorphic classification, offering an efficient solution for robotic perception and human-computer interaction.
