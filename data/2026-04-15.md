### [1] [Generative Anonymization in Event Streams](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12803)
*Adam T. Müller, Mihai Kocsis, Nicolaj C. Stache*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了首个面向事件流的生成式匿名化框架，通过弥合异步事件与标准空间生成模型之间的模态差距，在保护隐私的同时保持下游视觉任务的数据效用。该方法将事件投影到中间强度表示，利用预训练模型合成不存在的逼真身份，并将特征重新编码回神经形态域。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 神经形态视觉传感器在公共空间部署时引发严重的数据保护问题，现有事件到视频模型能够从稀疏事件流重建高保真强度图像，无意中暴露人类身份。当前混淆方法会破坏时空结构，严重降低下游感知任务的数据效用，因此需要解决这种效用与隐私之间的权衡问题。

**Method:** 该框架通过三个关键步骤实现：首先将异步事件流投影到中间强度表示以弥合模态差距，然后利用预训练生成模型合成逼真但不存在的身份特征，最后将这些特征重新编码回神经形态域。该方法还引入了通过精确机器人轨迹捕获的新型同步真实世界事件和RGB数据集，为隐私保护神经形态视觉研究提供稳健基准。

**Result:** 实验表明该方法能够可靠地防止从事件到视频重建中恢复身份，同时保持下游视觉任务所需的结构数据完整性。该方法在保护隐私的同时维持了事件流的时空特性，为神经形态视觉数据的安全处理提供了有效解决方案。

**Conclusion:** 该研究首次解决了事件流中的效用-隐私权衡问题，通过生成式匿名化框架实现了身份保护而不损害数据质量。引入的同步数据集为未来隐私保护神经形态视觉研究提供了标准化评估基准，推动了该领域向实际部署应用的发展。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Neuromorphic vision sensors offer low latency and high dynamic range, but their deployment in public spaces raises severe data protection concerns. Recent Event-to-Video (E2V) models can reconstruct high-fidelity intensity images from sparse event streams, inadvertently exposing human identities. Current obfuscation methods, such as masking or scrambling, corrupt the spatio-temporal structure, severely degrading data utility for downstream perception tasks. In this paper, to the best of our knowledge, we present the first generative anonymization framework for event streams to resolve this utility-privacy trade-off. By bridging the modality gap between asynchronous events and standard spatial generative models, our pipeline projects events into an intermediate intensity representation, leverages pretrained models to synthesize realistic, non-existent identities, and re-encodes the features back into the neuromorphic domain. Experiments demonstrate that our method reliably prevents identity recovery from E2V reconstructions while preserving the structural data integrity required for downstream vision tasks. Finally, to facilitate rigorous evaluation, we introduce a novel, synchronized real-world event and RGB dataset captured via precise robotic trajectories, providing a robust benchmark for future research in privacy-preserving neuromorphic vision.

### [2] [Adaptive Spiking Neurons for Vision and Language Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12365)
*Chenlin Zhou, Sihang Guo, Jiaqi Wang, Dongyang Ma, Jin Cheng, Qingyan Meng, Zhengyu Ma, Yonghong Tian*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出了自适应脉冲神经元（ASN）及其变体归一化自适应脉冲神经元（NASN），作为新一代通用脉冲神经元，通过可训练参数学习膜电位动态并实现自适应发放，在整数训练和脉冲推理范式下显著提升了SNN的性能、适应性和训练效率。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 随着大模型的发展，当前需要高性能、强适应性和高效训练的脉冲神经元，但现有脉冲神经元设计缺乏统一的理论指导，难以满足多样化任务需求，因此需要设计新一代通用脉冲神经元来解决这些局限性。

**Method:** 首先提出了设计新一代脉冲神经元的功能性视角作为通用指导原则，在此基础上提出了自适应脉冲神经元（ASN），该神经元引入可训练参数来学习膜电位动态并实现自适应发放，采用整数训练和脉冲推理范式以提高训练效率，并进一步提出了归一化自适应脉冲神经元（NASN）变体，通过集成归一化技术来稳定训练过程。

**Result:** 在涵盖视觉和语言模态的五类不同任务的19个数据集上进行了评估，实验结果表明ASN系列神经元在多种任务中表现出色，验证了其有效性和通用性，证明了该设计能够满足高性能、适应性和训练效率的需求。

**Conclusion:** ASN系列神经元代表了脉冲神经网络发展的新一代通用脉冲神经元，其功能性设计视角为未来神经元设计提供了理论框架，整数训练和自适应发放机制为高效SNN训练开辟了新途径，有望推动SNN在更广泛领域的应用。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
Regarded as the third generation of neural networks, Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) have garnered significant traction due to their biological plausibility and energy efficiency. Recent advancements in large models necessitate spiking neurons capable of high performance, adaptability, and training efficiency. In this work, we first propose a novel functional perspective that provides general guidance for designing the new generation of spiking neurons. Following the insightful guidelines, we propose the Adaptive Spiking Neuron (ASN), which incorporates trainable parameters to learn membrane potential dynamics and enable adaptive firing. ASN adopts an integer training and spike inference paradigm, facilitating efficient SNN training. To further enhance robustness, we propose a specialized variant of ASN, the Normalized Adaptive Spiking Neuron (NASN), which integrates normalization to stabilize training. We evaluate our neuron model on 19 datasets spanning five distinct tasks in both vision and language modalities, demonstrating the effectiveness and versatility of the ASN family. Our ASN family is expected to become the new generation of general-purpose spiking neurons.

### [3] [EMBER: Autonomous Cognitive Behaviour from Learned Spiking Neural Network Dynamics in a Hybrid LLM Architecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12167)
*William Savage*

#### 🧩 TL;DR
本文提出EMBER，一种混合认知架构，将LLM作为可替换的推理引擎置于基于生物启发的持久关联基质中，实现了无需外部触发的自主推理与行动。该架构通过22万神经元脉冲神经网络与STDP机制，实现了LLM的自主激活与关联驱动决策。

---

#### 📘 Detailed Summary
**Motivation:** 当前LLM增强方法主要依赖检索工具扩展模型能力，但缺乏生物合理的记忆与推理分离架构。本研究旨在重新组织LLM与记忆的关系，构建基于生物启发的持久关联基质，使LLM成为可替换的推理引擎，实现自主触发和关联驱动的决策过程。

**Method:** 提出EMBER混合认知架构，核心为22万神经元脉冲神经网络，采用STDP可塑性、四层层次结构（感觉/概念/类别/元模式）、抑制性E/I平衡和奖励调节学习。文本嵌入通过新颖的z-score标准化top-k群体编码转换为维度无关的表示，LLM作为可替换推理引擎集成于该生物启发的关联基质中。

**Result:** 系统在零初始权重下仅需7次对话交换（14条消息）即产生首次SNN触发行动，文本嵌入编码在多种维度下保持82.2%的辨别保留率。STDP横向传播能在空闲操作期间自主触发LLM行动，无需外部提示或脚本触发，系统在8小时空闲期后基于学习到的人物-主题关联自主发起用户联系。

**Conclusion:** 该研究展示了生物启发关联基质与LLM集成的新范式，实现了自主推理与行动触发，为构建更接近生物认知的AI系统提供了可行路径。架构的可替换推理引擎设计为不同LLM集成提供了灵活性，STDP驱动的自主决策机制为具身智能和持续学习系统开辟了新方向。

---

#### 📄 Abstract
We present (Experience-Modulated Biologically-inspired Emergent Reasoning), a hybrid cognitive architecture that reorganises the relationship between large language models (LLMs) and memory: rather than augmenting an LLM with retrieval tools, we place the LLM as a replaceable reasoning engine within a persistent, biologically-grounded associative substrate.
  The architecture centres on a 220,000-neuron spiking neural network (SNN) with spike-timing-dependent plasticity (STDP), four-layer hierarchical organisation (sensory/concept/category/meta-pattern), inhibitory E/I balance, and reward-modulated learning. Text embeddings are encoded into the SNN via a novel z-score standardised top-k population code that is dimension-independent by construction, achieving 82.2\% discrimination retention across embedding dimensionalities.
  We show that STDP lateral propagation during idle operation can trigger and shape LLM actions without external prompting or scripted triggers: the SNN determines when to act and what associations to surface, while the LLM selects the action type and generates content. In one instance, the system autonomously initiated contact with a user after learned person-topic associations fired laterally during an 8-hour idle period. From a clean start with zero learned weights, the first SNN-triggered action occurred after only 7 conversational exchanges (14 messages).
